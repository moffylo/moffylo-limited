MOFFYLO LIMITED

We specialize in supplying and distributing a diverse range of high-quality animal feeds to farmers, livestock owners, and agricultural businesses. Our product offerings include wheat bran, broken wheat, and wheat shaft, ensuring optimal nutrition for livestock.

These feeds are formulated to promote healthy growth, enhance productivity, and improve overall animal well-being. We are committed to providing consistent, reliable, and efficient delivery services to meet the specific needs of our clients.
