MOFFYLO LIMITED

Our service specializes in the efficient collection and recycling of a wide variety of scrap materials, contributing to a more sustainable and eco-friendly future. We handle diverse materials, including metals, oils, electronics (such as computer accessories), batteries, vehicles (including trucks), electric copper, stainless steels, and aluminum, ensuring that each type is processed appropriately and responsibly.
By recycling scrap materials, we help reduce the environmental impact associated with waste disposal and promote the circular economy. Our processes are designed to recover valuable resources from waste, transforming discarded materials into reusable raw materials. This not only conserves natural resources but also reduces energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions associated with the production of new materials.

We adhere to strict environmental regulations and industry standards to ensure that our collection and recycling methods are safe and sustainable. Our commitment to responsible recycling practices means that we prioritize environmentally friendly processes that minimize waste and promote resource recovery.
In addition to our collection services, we provide our clients with valuable insights into their scrap materials, helping them understand the potential value of their waste. We aim to foster long-term partnerships with our clients by providing exceptional service, transparency, and reliability in all aspects of our operations.
