MOFFYLO LIMITED

We specialize in the distribution of high-quality flours specifically formulated for animal feed. These include wheat by-products such as wheat middlings, wheat bran, and other flour residues, which are rich in essential nutrients and ideal for promoting the health and productivity of livestock.

Our animal feed flours are carefully selected to meet the nutritional needs of various livestock, providing a cost-effective and sustainable solution for farmers and livestock owners. By repurposing flour by-products into feed, we help support sustainable agricultural practices while ensuring the healthy growth and development of animals. Whether you manage a small farm or a large-scale agricultural operation, we are committed to delivering top-quality feed solutions to meet your needs.
