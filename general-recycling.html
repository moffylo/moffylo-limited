MOFFYLO LIMITED

Our service encompasses the comprehensive collection, repurposing, and recycling of a wide range of recyclable materials with the goal of reducing waste, conserving natural resources, and promoting environmental sustainability. We specialize in handling materials that can be reintroduced into the production cycle, turning waste into valuable resources.
We manage the collection and recycling of metals, plastics, paper, wood, glass, electronics, and other materials from industrial, commercial, and residential sources. By ensuring that these materials are processed correctly, we reduce the volume of waste that ends up in landfills, minimizing environmental impact. Our service plays a vital role in mitigating pollution, lowering energy consumption, and cutting greenhouse gas emissions through efficient recycling practices.

Our recycling process involves stringent sorting and handling to maximize the recovery rate and ensure that only high-quality materials are reintroduced into the production chain. We utilize advanced recycling technologies and adhere to industry standards to ensure that materials are recycled efficiently, safely, and responsibly.
